GPS Device Setup Instructions

If you are a member of USA Channel 22 and would like to have your device added to the GPS Server, please follow the instructions below. If you have questions, ask stere0123.

  • 1. Download the app

    Download "Traccar Client" (white/green circle icon) in the app store on Android or iPhone.

  • 2. Open the app and configure it

    Once the app is installed, open it and change the following settings:
    • Server URL = http://gps.ch22.us:5055
    • Frequency = your choice (in seconds) (common choices: 5sec, 15sec, 30sec - choose depending on activity)
    • Location provider = GPS provider

  • 3. Give stere0123 your Device Identifier number

    Your Device Identifier number (found near the top of the app) identifies your device to the server. Give this number to stere0123 so your device may be added.

  • 4. Get turned on!

    The service on/off toggle is at the top of the app. Use this whenever you want to enable or disable GPS tracking.
