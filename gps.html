USA Channel 22 GPS Server

Previously, we hosted our own GPS server (powered by Traccar) as a fun project to voluntarily share and track the locations of our members.

Due to issues with server stability and upkeep, this service is no longer active as of 2018-10-27.

All data has been archived and can be extracted if requested.

Google Maps Real-time Location Sharing

If you are still interested in sharing your location with others, please use Google Maps Real-time Location Sharing.

This will allow you to share your location directly with any number of members/friends/family, or with our main Google account for other members to see.

To view the shared locations of other members, just login to our main Google account and go to Google Maps, or have them share their location directly to your own account.
