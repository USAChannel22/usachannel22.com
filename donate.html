Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 3

Core/HC Anything Anywhere

Any killstreak will be unlocked after said number of kills in one life, excempt from Hardline, and is only reset upon death.

These bonuses are subject to change, however you are likely to become grandfathered into changes, and if one must be taken then it will not be taken until after a full subscription cycle.
Level 1 (Broke Ass)
You can choose one tier 1 perk, OR one tier 1 killstreak.

Level 2 (Kind Gentleman)
You can choose one of any available perk, AND one of any available killstreak.

Level 3 (Nitro)
You can choose one of any available perk, AND one of any available killstreak, AND one extra tier 1 perk or killstreak.

Level 4 (Dollar an Inch Club)
You can choose one of any available perk, AND one of any available killstreak, AND one extra of any available perk or killstreak.
