Call of Duty Server

Incase people are too ******* stupid to connect the dots, this server uses Call of Duty maps.

The idea is any and every (working) CoD map will be in the rotations here. For most, VR is a new experiance, but VR on a map we all already know and love is a whole new experiance in of it's self. At the time this is written, we currently have 17 maps lined up for rotation, from Cod 2, World at War, Modern Warfare, Modern Warfare 2, Modern Warfare 3, Black Ops, Black ops 2, and Infinite Warfare.

These maps are remade or converted in by other talented members of the Pavlov VR community
